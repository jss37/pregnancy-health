Why is oral health in pregnancy so important?

Healthy mouths reduce gum disease and support healthy birth outcomes. Evidence shows that dental care - including x-rays with shielding, local anesthetics, antibiotics when indicated - is safe through pregnancy.

  • Lower risk of tooth decay
  • Fewer missed days
  • Early childhood cavity
